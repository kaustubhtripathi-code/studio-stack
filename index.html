Snapline

A native screenshot companion that lives on a global hotkey. Press Ctrl + Alt + S anywhere, drag a box, annotate, and it's on your clipboard — perfect for client feedback and proposals.

  • Global Ctrl+Alt+S hotkey — no window switching
  • Rectangle, arrow, freehand pen & text annotations

  • Self-contained .exe · multi-monitor & HiDPI aware
